REPORT ON MOMENTUM CHROME EXTENSION

1. INTRODUCTION

1.1 Purpose

Momentum is a to-do-list extension that can be used with the Google Chrome browser; it replaces the New Tab page with a personal dashboard that features a to-do list, weather, and inspirational quotes that change daily. It is designed for people who spend a lot of time in their browser opening new tabs.

2. PROBLEM

For some people, it may be difficult to be productive constantly and to stay focused on work and with homework. With the capabilities that Internet browsers have with surfing the Web, it can lead to major distractions while using Chrome to do productive work.

3. BENEFITS

The benefits that I found with using the Momentum Chrome extension are as follows:

  • Inspiring quotes — Quotes from revered scholars (feat. Booker T. Washington, Leonardo da Vinci, and more) helped me to feel productive and to feel inspired as I worked on homework and studied.
  • Clear goals in mind — To-do list gave me a visual guide that helped me to keep track of my tasks and goals.
  • Simple design — User-friendly and simple design of the Momentum Chrome extension made it much easier for me to be able to add and track tasks, improving my productivity.
